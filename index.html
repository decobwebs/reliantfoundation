The Foundation

Reliant Anchor Foundation (IT: 8437902) is a dedicated non-profit focused on inclusive educational opportunities. Our vision is to break the cycle of poverty by equipping individuals with technical skills necessary to contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's development.

Aim

To provide accessible and inclusive educational opportunities for underprivileged individuals in Nigeria, focusing on logistics and IT.

Values

Equality, empowerment, and industrial excellence through quality education and essential learning resources.
